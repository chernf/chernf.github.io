Professor Fei Chen

Professor, College of AI, Nankai University (Tianjin, China)

Associate Dean, College of AI, Nankai University

Principal Investigator, Laboratory for Information and Control Systems (LICS)

  • Email: fchen@nankai.edu.cn

Research focuses on networked systems with an emphasis on distributed cooperative control, distributed optimization, and distributed learning.

Research interests

Distributed cooperative control

Coordination of multi-agent systems under network constraints: consensus, formation, and cooperation.

Distributed optimization

Scalable optimization methods over networks with communication efficiency and theoretical guarantees.

Distributed learning

Learning over networks (decentralized/federated settings) with reliable updates and performance analysis.
